Exercise25: In this exercise you will be turning on LEDs two at a time from bottom to top (just like last time), then go backwards from top to bottom.

ACTIVITIES:

  1. Copy your completed Exercise24 into the Arduino IDE.
  2. Compile and upload this program and verify that LED’s 2 & 9, 3 & 10, 4 & 11, and so on up through LED’s 8 & 15 blink on and off. This then repeats five times. Then the programs pauses four seconds with all LEDS OFF and then the whole thing starts over again….
  3. Modify the program by adding another for loop that counts backwards from 7 – 3 blinking LED’s 7 & 14 on and off together, then 6 & 13 together and so on down through LED’s 10 & 3 together. Then repeat this sequence five times. Then the program pauses for 4 seconds and the whole thing starts over again....
  4. Make Sure you are using variables in your delays. For example: delay(waitTime); instead of delay(100);.
  5. Save the completed sketch as YourInitials_Exercise25 in: Documents – Arduino – Exercises
  6. Copy the code into the Giffer website
  7. Enter your name and exercise number.
  8. Select the Run and Grade button.
  9. Look at the resulting Output image and make sure it is correct (Correct at lower part of image)
  10. If it is Correct -  use theCopy Page button to copy the evidence that you have correctly completed the exercise to clipboard.
  11. Go to CL 10.3.25 Upload Completed Exercise25:  And upload evidence that you have completed Exercise25
  12. Click "Prepare an answer" on NEO
  13. Click the "<>" (Source code) button.
  14. Paste by pressing CTRL + V
  15. Click "Save and submit for grading"
  16. If you made changes to the code in Giffer, then Copy the code from Giffer and paste it into the Arduino and re-save it as: YourInitials_Exercise25 - in your Documents/Arduino/Exercises Folder.
