A program that does nothing.

 

NEW:  void setup()

DESCRIPTION:  setup happens ONLY once under the following conditions:

  1. A) When the Arduino is Turned on
  2. B) When the Arduino Reset – find the reset button
  3. C) When downloading a program to the Arduino and resetting the Arduino
  4. D) Notice that D13 flashes 3 times to indicate successful reset

 

NEW:  pinMode(2, OUTPUT);

DESCRIPTION: Enables pin 2 on the Arduino to Send enough power o turn on LED attached to pin 2

Usage - now the arduino MAY (enabled / allowed) turn on the LED attached to pin2. This instruction however does not tell the arduino to turn on the LED attached to pin 2. That instruction is digitalWrite(2,HIGH) and we will talk about that shortly.

 

NEW:  void loop()

DESCRIPTION: loop() happens OVER and OVER again forever AFTER setup() finishes:

 

NEW:  delay(5000);

DESCRIPTION:  Tells Arduino to wait 5000ms or 5 seconds before going to the next line
